My Story

Why I Started Mathias Auto Locksmith

I set up Mathias Auto Locksmith because I believed local drivers in Basingstoke deserved a faster, more honest alternative to the big national companies. When you are locked out of your car at midnight or you have lost your only key, the last thing you need is to be passed between call handlers and wait hours for someone to show up.

When you call me, you speak to me directly. I give you an honest quote over the phone, I come to you, I do the work, and I will not leave until the job is done.

No surprises. No subcontractors. No nonsense.
