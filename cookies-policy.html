Cookies Policy

Information about how cookies are used on the Mathias Auto Locksmith website.

Last updated: June 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They allow the website to remember certain information about your visit and can improve your experience on future visits.

How We Use Cookies

The Mathias Auto Locksmith website is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages and served via Cloudflare. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies of our own.

Our contact form is powered by Basin (usebasin.com). Basin may place a strictly necessary session cookie to support the secure submission of form data. This cookie does not track you across other websites and is not used for advertising or profiling purposes.

Cloudflare, our content delivery and security provider, may set a cookie called __cf_bm to distinguish between humans and automated traffic. This is a security measure and is strictly necessary for the protection of the website.

Third-Party Cookies

We embed no third-party content (such as social media widgets, YouTube videos, or advertising networks) that would set cookies from external services. Social media links in our footer are standard outbound links and do not cause cookies to be set by those platforms on our website.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. The following links provide guidance for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the contact form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
